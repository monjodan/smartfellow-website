What the review includes

A serious piece of work, not a sales meeting.

We take the same time and care we would on a paid engagement. The result is a written plan you can use internally. Even if you decide the right answer is to wait.

Workshop with your team

A structured 90-minute conversation with the people who would actually use the system. We listen more than we talk.

Systems and data walkthrough

A quick look at your current tools, document types, and sensitivity requirements. We flag what should stay local and what could be outside.

Two or three user interviews

Short calls with two or three of the people who do the work today. We find where AI would actually save time, and where it would just get in the way.

Written report and fixed quote

A document identifying highest-leverage workflows, the right on-prem architecture for your sensitivity needs, and. If we recommend building. A fixed quote.

Who this is for

If any of these sound familiar, the review will be useful.

Your data is too sensitive for cloud AI

Regulated industries, confidential client material, trade secrets, or personal data that cannot leave your environment by policy.

You have real documents and real workflows

Years of internal documentation and well-defined processes that an AI system could learn, rather than a blank slate.

You want to own the system, not rent one

You prefer a one-time build with predictable maintenance over a recurring subscription to someone else's platform.

A note on honesty

If after the review we believe a cloud tool would genuinely serve you better, or that the timing is wrong, we will say so. The review is free precisely so neither side has to pretend otherwise.
