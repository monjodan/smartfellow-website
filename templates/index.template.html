Positioning

This is a managed system for firms that need control, not spectacle.

The point is not to add another dashboard. The point is to give your team a working AI colleague that fits into existing channels, keeps context local, and becomes more useful as it learns your standards.

Works in familiar channels

Your team speaks to Smart Fellow in Slack, Teams, KakaoTalk, or email. No new interface, no user training, no adoption campaign.

Learns your way of working

It picks up terminology, formatting, red flags, and internal preferences over time, so the output gets closer to your standard every week.

Keeps the base system local

Documents, memory, and business context stay on the machine in your environment by default. External models are optional, not foundational.
