Where we say no

Smart Fellow isn't right for everyone.

We're honest about who we don't help. If any of these is you, ChatGPT, a SaaS subscription, or a Big Four contract will serve you better. We'll be the first to say so.

Fortune 500s and government bodies

You need a procurement process, an enterprise vendor, and a security review we don't run. Talk to Samsung SDS, LG CNS, or the Korean arm of a global integrator.

DIY builders

If you want to spin up models on your own GPU and fine-tune them yourself, do that. We're for people who want a partner running the whole thing, not a toolkit to assemble.

"AI for the next investor meeting"

If you want a demo to show a board, this isn't the shape. We build operational systems that take six weeks to install. Not press releases.
