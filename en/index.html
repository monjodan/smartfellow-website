No technical knowledge needed. No IT department required. We handle everything.

The idea is simple

Think of it as hiring someone. Not buying software.

You onboard a new colleague. They learn your business, your processes, your way of doing things, and they keep getting better. Unlike software, they don't sit in a dashboard waiting to be configured. They work, just like anyone else on your team.

Works where you work

No new app to learn. It comes to you.

Your Smart Fellow joins the conversations your team is already having in Slack, Telegram, KakaoTalk, or email. You message it the same way you would message any colleague.

No dashboards. No logins to manage. No training sessions for your staff. It's simply there, ready to help.

Gets better every week

It learns your business. Every single day.

Each conversation makes your Smart Fellow more useful. It remembers your clients' names, your team's preferences, your way of working. It learns what good work looks like in your company.

After a few weeks, it starts anticipating what you need before you ask. Just like a great employee would.

How a Fellow joins your team

From your decision to your Fellow's first day. No surprises, no hidden complexity.

Week 1

Setup & first workflows

Role definition, access setup, communication preferences. Your Fellow handles its first real tasks and starts learning your business context.

Week 2

Calibration

Refinement based on early output review. Tone, format, and priorities are dialed in. Your Fellow starts to feel like it understands how your team works.

Ongoing

Gets sharper over time

Memory improves, workflows sharpen, new tasks can be added as your team discovers new use cases. The longer they stay, the better they get.

Questions you're probably asking

"What if it doesn't deliver?"

No long-term lock-in. Cancel with 60 days notice. We work closely with you during onboarding to make sure your Fellow is right for the role.

"Will my team actually use it?"

Your Fellow works in Slack, KakaoTalk, Teams — the apps your team already uses every day. No new tool to learn. No training required.

"Is our data safe?"

Private Fellows run 100% on local hardware. Cloud Fellows keep your files on your machine — only task requests touch the cloud. Either way, you control everything.

"How fast will we see results?"

Most teams get useful output in week one. By week two, your Fellow is calibrated to your tone, your formats, and your way of doing things.
