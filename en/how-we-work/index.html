Our engagement model

Four phases. One team.

We don't sell platform access. We plan, build, and maintain your private AI system. Here's how that works.

01 Assess Free · 48-hour turnaround
02 Design & quote Fixed scope · Fixed price
03 Build & integrate 4–12 weeks typical
04 Maintain & evolve Monthly partnership

Phase 01 · Assess

A written plan for your private AI team. No cost. No commitment.

The AI Readiness Review is a short, structured engagement: a workshop with your team, a walkthrough of the systems and documents an AI team would touch, and conversations with two or three of the people who would actually use it. We leave you with a written report and, if it's worth building, a scoped quote.

The point is not to sell you something. The point is to tell you honestly whether a private AI team would help, where it would help, and what it would take. Some readiness reviews end with a recommendation to do nothing. That is a fine outcome.

At a glance
Cost Free
Duration 48 hours from workshop to report
Your time A 90-minute workshop, a few interviews
Deliverable Written report + quote
What you get A document identifying your highest-leverage workflows, the right on-prem architecture for your sensitivity requirements, risks to be aware of, and. If we recommend moving forward. A fixed-scope quote for the build and the monthly maintenance.

Phase 02 · Design & quote

A fixed-scope proposal before anything is built.

If you decide to move forward, we translate the readiness review into a concrete build proposal. Architecture choices, hardware specification, the integrations we will wire up, the documents we will fine-tune on, the workflows we will implement, the timeline, and a fixed price.

No hourly billing, no open-ended statements of work. You know what you are agreeing to before anything starts. If the scope changes later, we price the change and you decide whether to proceed.

At a glance
Pricing model Fixed quote
Scope Tailored to your workflows
What you sign One clear engagement letter
Turnaround Within two weeks of the review
What you get A concrete design: reference architecture, hardware specification, integration plan, fine-tuning plan, timeline, acceptance criteria, and a single fixed price for the build. No hidden modules, no later surprises.

Phase 03 · Build & integrate

Hardware on-site. Models fine-tuned on your data. Integrated into the tools your team already uses.

We procure and configure the hardware, fine-tune local models on your documents and workflows, and integrate the system into the channels your team already works in. Slack, KakaoTalk, Teams, email, or your internal systems. Everything is installed on-site and verified in your environment.

You own the hardware. You own the model weights. You own the data. When the build is done, you have a private AI system that continues to belong to you even if our relationship ends.

At a glance
Typical duration 4–12 weeks
Deployment On-site, on your hardware
Ownership Hardware, weights, and data: yours
Integrations Slack, KakaoTalk, Teams, email, internal systems
What you get A working private AI system on hardware in your office, integrated into the tools your team already uses, with documented acceptance tests, an admin handover, and trained users.

Phase 04 · Maintain & evolve

A monthly partnership. So the system stays sharp.

Once the system is live, we stay on. Monitoring, model updates, security patches, and tuning as your workflows evolve. When you want the system to learn a new skill or work with a new process, we train it: no new engagement, no new statement of work.

The maintenance fee is flat and predictable: designed to feel like an ongoing partnership, not a recurring invoice. You can end the relationship at any time; the system still belongs to you.

At a glance
Billing Flat monthly
Includes Monitoring, updates, patches, tuning
New skills Added within the partnership
Termination Any time; the system stays yours
What you get A system that stays current, a team on call when something changes, and the freedom to end the partnership without losing what was built. Your private AI team keeps working, regardless.

A note on price

Build cost and monthly maintenance are both quoted after the free AI Readiness Review, because both depend on scope. We do not publish a menu because there isn't one, but we do commit to fixed prices, no usage fees, and no surprise invoices. If your budget is a concern, tell us during the review and we will design to it.
